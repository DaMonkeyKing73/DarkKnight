About Dark Knight Chess Club

Our club is primarily focused on teaching beginners through three monthly classes and helping new players build solid fundamentals.

  • Primarily focused on beginners and fundamentals
  • Family-friendly atmosphere with youth classes
  • Flexible membership options and drop-in play

Our Mission

To create a strong local chess community focused on teaching beginners through quality monthly classes.

What You'll Find at the Club

Monthly Classes

Three beginner-focused classes each month that build fundamentals and confidence focused on quality learning.
